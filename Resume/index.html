Two
industries

I've always enjoyed both math and writing. In high school, I created a newspaper club called The Informant even as I devoured material in a Physics Honors class. In undergraduate, I showed up for a computer engineering degree, but sought out the student paper the first week I was on campus. I threw my heart and soul into The Advocate News team even as I became President of the school's robotics club. I used journalism to pay my rent in the summer. When I graduated, I took a software consultant job, but wished I had the time to pick up stories in the evenings. Now, I'm attending graduate school for journalism so that I can do either and feel confident in my skills. I hope to always keep both industries close.
